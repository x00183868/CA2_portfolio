HSE Internship Experience

AI & Automation Centre of Excellence (CoE)

Overview

During my internship with the HSE AI & Automation Centre of Excellence (CoE), I worked across digital automation, AI governance, workflow design, and RPA operations. I gained hands-on experience building automation solutions, assessing AI systems for compliance, and supporting operational teams across national healthcare services.

Power Automate Development

I designed and built automation workflows using Microsoft Power Automate. This included extracting Microsoft Forms responses, building variables, processing data, and automatically adding structured rows to Excel tables stored in OneDrive for Business.

  • Microsoft Forms → Power Automate → Excel integration
  • Use of triggers, response parsing, and variable creation
  • Workflow automation for HSE Interim Assessments

RPA Operations & Incident Support

I gained exposure to HSE's robotic process automation environment, learning how automated workflows operate across financial and clinical systems. I reviewed RPA failure cases within Dynamics 365 and learned how incidents are escalated and resolved.

  • Understanding UiPath selector failures
  • Incident life cycle: Identification → Development → Testing
  • Working within Dynamics 365 case management

AI Governance & Compliance

I supported AI governance activities by working with the HSE AI Inventory, ensuring systems met national and EU AI Act requirements. This involved completing structured AI assessment templates and ensuring ethical, safe, and transparent AI use.

  • AI Inventory registration
  • Risk categorisation (Minimal, Limited, High-risk)
  • Awareness of DPIA / FRIA requirements
  • Understanding Responsible AI principles

Strategic Project Exposure

I worked with documentation for the Sláintecare Integration Innovation Fund (SIIF), reviewing AI strategic priority templates. This allowed me to understand how AI projects are evaluated, prioritised, and delivered within a national healthcare setting.

  • Problem and impact analysis
  • Stakeholder mapping and evaluation planning
  • AI delivery models: Buy, Build, Guidance
